Core features

One app, every patch of dirt.

Today-first

Open the app and the day is already sorted. Every enterprise's tasks, the weather, and anything overdue land on one morning agenda, laid right over your property map.

Voice + AI logging

Hold the button and talk. “Collected fourteen eggs from coop north.” Steadbook turns plain speech into a clean, filed entry on-device. No audio ever leaves your phone.

Every enterprise, one Stead

Animals, perennials, annuals, propagation, compost, vines, brewing, forestry and aquaculture — each with screens drawn for how that enterprise actually works, indexed and switchable by pattern, status or season.

Compliance built in

BeeWhere, PUR, Prop 12, CDFA. Steadbook detects what your county requires, pre-fills it from your logs, and surfaces filing windows before they close.

Set up your stead

Pick your county and your enterprises and the rest configures itself — compliance and seasons included, restored from your iCloud. You're logging within minutes of opening the app.

Compliance & privacy

Records you own. Audit-ready.

No accounts. No server. No telemetry. Your stead lives in your own private database and leaves as cleanly as it came in, soil to label.

Yours, in your iCloud

No accounts or servers. Records live in your own private database.

Audit-ready logs

Every entry timestamped and traceable from the harvest back to the label.

County notifications

Filing windows and spray rules detected from your county, pre-filled from logs.

Export anytime

Take your full record set with you in open formats, no lock-in.
